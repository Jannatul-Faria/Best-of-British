BEST OF BRITISH

About Us

Best of British is a unique platform designed to connect local stores with Buyers, Sellers, and Recommenders across the UK. Our goal is to bridge the gap between high-street shops and customers both near and far by leveraging the power of personal recommendations and live video shopping experiences.

We believe in supporting small and medium-sized businesses while offering Buyers a convenient way to discover and purchase items recommended by people they trust. Whether you're a Seller looking to reach more customers, a Buyer seeking personal shopping experiences, or a recommender sharing products with friends and family, Best of British empowers you to engage in meaningful, seamless transactions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revitalise local commerce by providing a modern, tech-savvy platform where Buyers, Sellers, and Recommenders can thrive together.

We aim to:

  • Empower Local Businesses: Giving high-street and independent stores tools to reach a wider audience.
  • Create Personal Connections: Allowing Buyers to experience live shopping and recommendations from people they trust.
  • Foster Community: Building a network where shoppers, Sellers, and Recommenders can support each other.

At Best of British, we’re committed to making shopping personal, trustworthy, and community-driven while enhancing the experience for everyone involved.
